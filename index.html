About the Ranch

A Passion for the Longhorn Breed

RLG Longhorns, LLC is a small, family-run operation located in Cameron, Texas, dedicated to raising quality registered Texas Longhorn breeding stock. With five years of hands-on experience and a commitment to the breed, every animal in our herd is TLBAA registered and raised with care.

We take pride in the bloodlines we've built and the animals we produce. Whether you're looking to start your own herd or add quality genetics to an existing one, we'd love to talk cattle.
